Context

In Q1 2021, roughly six months after having started to work on our new web platform, we turned our attention to the mobile app. This time weâ€™re not starting a new codebase, but building on top of the existing one.

We had two major goals with this project:

  1. 1. Redefine the app structure to allow for more flexibility and for the introduction of brand new concepts (e.g. capturing leads outside the context of an event).
  2. 2. Elevate the visual design to a higher standard.

Discovery and Ideation

As usual, we started out by laying down very rough (and I mean rough) screens with all the interactions needed to map the underlying structure and then creating wireframe prototypes that we could put in front of people and start gathering feedback.

Outcome

After having gathered tonnes of feedback, we finally started working on refining the visuals and the interactions.

Here are some of the highlights:

The home screen was redesigned to allow for quick lead captures outside the context of an event and to bring forward relevant and contextual information.

We packed the event view with more information, but in a way that isn't too overwhelming for our users.

The collection method selection was completely redesigned to separate it from the collection form and make for an easier experience.

The form was updated to highlight lead information and separated into different, logical sections to simplify the capture process.

This project is actively being worked on and will be released later in the year.
